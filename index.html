Add to Home Screen

Install this app to your home screen for easy access:

Open the Safari browser and open MSD Oncology web app (oncology.msdconnect.sg). Now you can add the app to your iOS Home Screen by following steps as below:

1

Press the share button

2

Select “Add to Home Screen”

3

Press the “Add” button

Eventually you should be able to find the newly installed Oncology App on your Home Screen.
